Here is what you should know

sisiHub is a tech startup, co-founded by Bernard Ouma, Chris Otta, Herons Odhiambo and Issa Athman, hatched from the idea of creating an accessible meeting point for the growing IT community around Maseno. It adopts a collaborative working structure and creates space for technology community to come and work together.

We would love to work with you or if you want to support us, please feel free to click the button.

What we do

Mentorship

At these forums students and participants for these events are grouped based on their interesting technology or interests then are taught on the basics of the discipline.

Proffessionals from these disciplines teach the students the prerequisites of being active in the market and ways of staying alive in the business.

Collaboration

We believe that bundling resources not only saves cost, but working together in one space with many co-workers is seemed as a source of inspiration and creates collaborative possibilities on a project based level.

sisiHub aims to create the very space, offering learning, networking and collaboration opportunities as well as workspace for individuals small startups for the Maseno University technology community and its environs.

Flexible Workspaces

Based on the idea of open membership and the provision of access to technology, we host tech meet-ups bringing together tech enthusiast from the student population to share resources, knowledge and jointly work on IT related projects.

Collaboration takes an informal approach as is the atmosphere.
